Project A
Country & Committee Assignment (Individual)

Your Assignment

Each student will be assigned:

  • One Country: e.g., India, Brazil, USA, Kenya, Norway, Japan
  • One Committee: UNGA / UNHRC / WHO / UNICEF
  • One Agenda:
    • Climate Change and Sustainable Development
    • Access to Healthcare in Developing Nations
    • Refugee Protection and Human Rights
    • Cybersecurity and Global Safety

Deliverable 1: Country Profile Sheet (1 page)

Create a one-page profile in Google Docs or Word and submit it via the LMS.

Include the following:

  • Basic facts about the country: location, population, economy
  • Political system and global role
  • Country’s past actions or stance related to the agenda
  • One challenge the country faces regarding the issue
