1

Committees

Committees replicate real UN bodies where delegates debate global issues.

  • UNGA – Global policy discussions
  • UNHRC – Human rights & crises
  • WHO – Health & epidemics
  • UNSC – Peace & security
  • UNICEF, UNDP & more
Explore Special Committees
  • Indian committees (Lok Sabha, AIPPM)
  • Historic committees (Mahabharata, LoN)
  • Fiction-based (Harry Potter, Avengers)
  • International Press & Media
2

Delegates

Every participant represents a country — not themselves.

  • Speak from your country’s official stance
  • Research foreign policy & alliances
  • Defend national interests diplomatically
3

Agenda

The core issue debated throughout the committee.

  • UNGA – Equitable representation reforms
  • UNHRC – Refugee rights & crises
  • UNSC – Cybersecurity threats
4

Executive Board

Chairperson, Vice-Chair & Rapporteur oversee proceedings.

They evaluate speeches, diplomacy, and participation.

5

Chits

Written communication tools for strategy and procedure.

  • Direct chits for lobbying & alliances
  • Via-EB chits for points & procedure
  • Substantive chits influence scoring

Tip: Clear, concise chits = silent diplomacy.
