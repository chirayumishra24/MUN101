Project B

Country Profile Presentation (Video Submission)

1

Prepare Your Country Profile

Use the 1-page Word/Google Docs file you created in Project A (or update it if needed).

2

Record a 1–2 Minute Video

In the video, include:

  • Introduce yourself and your country
  • Share 3–4 key points from your profile
  • Explain your country’s stance/challenge
  • Speak clearly and confidently

Tip: Record in a quiet, well-lit place. Phone camera is fine!

3

Upload Your Video

Upload to:

  • YouTube (Unlisted or Public – recommended)
  • Instagram Reels (Public)

Tag @skillizee in the caption/description

4

Submit Your Assignment

In the LMS assignment template, paste the

direct link to your uploaded video

(YouTube link or Instagram post link)

Quick Recording Tips

  • • Find a quiet, well-lit place
  • • Place your phone/camera at eye level
  • • Smile and speak clearly – imagine you're addressing the UN!
  • • Keep it natural – 1–2 minutes is perfect
  • • Review once before uploading
