How This Activity Works

1

You'll be assigned a country and committee in a simulated MUN scenario.

2

Negotiate with AI delegates representing other nations to build consensus.

3

Draft a resolution and practice real diplomatic skills in a safe environment.

Tips for Success

  • • Stay in character — represent your country's true position
  • • Use formal MUN language (e.g., "The delegate of…")
  • • Listen to other delegates before proposing solutions
  • • Aim for consensus — diplomacy is about compromise
  • • Have fun — this is your chance to practice real-world negotiation!
